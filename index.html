🧮 How is this Math?

This game applies Discrete Mathematics and Formal Logic. You just used deductive reasoning to interpret constraints (the clues). By processing those conditions (like "FINACC1 is last") and eliminating impossible schedules (a math concept called proof by exhaustion), you solved a logical puzzle mathematically—just like solving for variables in an equation!
